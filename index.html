Welcome to my personal website!

I am a Hale Visiting Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics, Georgia Institute of Technology, hosted by Prof. Molei Tao and Prof. Santosh Vempala. Before joining Georgia Institute of Technology, I received my PhD. degree in Mathematics from University of California, Davis advised by Prof. Krishna Balasubramanian. My research focuses on applying probabilistic and Partial Differential Equations (PDE) tools to understand sampling and stochastic optimization algorithms used in Machine Learning (ML).
